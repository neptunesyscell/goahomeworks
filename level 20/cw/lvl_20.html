Comparisons and Logical Operators

1) Comparison Operators Examples:

  • 5 == 5: True
  • 5 != 6: True
  • 5 < 10: True

2) Logical Operators Examples:

  • (5 == 5 and 10 > 5): True
  • (5 == 5 or 10 < 5): True
  • (5 == 6 and 10 < 5): False

Working with Lists

1) List Length Example:

  • Length: 6

2) List Indexing:

  • Index 0: 1
  • Index 1: 2
  • Index 2: 3
  • Index 5: 6
